Jellof rice

This dish is a very popular Nigerian food commonly eaten on Sundays or Celebrations. It is the perfect go to food in any situations food is needed.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato
  • Seasonings (maggi, salt, pepper, etc)
  • Cooking oil
  • Water
  • Meat (Optional )

Instructions

  1. Wash and parboil the rice
  2. In hot oil, fry the onions, add the tomato paste and allow to cook for at least 5-10 mins
  3. Add your seasoning and enough water(or meat stock) into the pot
  4. Add the rice, mix it and allow it to cook
  5. When the rice is almost dry, add in your meat
  6. When dry, serve while hot
